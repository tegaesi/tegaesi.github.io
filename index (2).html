About Me

As a data analyst and data scientist, I am passionate about discovering hidden patterns in data and communicating insights. I enjoy these roles because through my work, I ease decision-making processes, leading to optimized operations, increased bottom-lines and higher levels of customer satisfaction.

Yes, I love the sitting behind my PC, writing code and building dashboards. But beyond that, I relish the interpersonal part of what I do, which includes making presentations on data insights, collaborating with stakeholders and teaching the use of analytics tools/concepts.

My Data Path

Really, all the activities on my analytics journey won’t fit in here. But here’s an attempt to show the highlights.

Oct '19

Compulsory National Youth Service

Completed my service year at Dangote Cement Company as an Intern Production Engineer (given my Bachelors in Chemical Engineering). Through the routine data entry associated with my role, I became aware of the relevance of data in industrial operations, and chose to pivot my career in this direction.

Jan '20

Data Analytics With Python

Began learning Python on Dataquest via their Under-represented Genders Scholarship. I also took my first deep-dive into data visualization with Power BI.

Jul '20

Online Analytics Bootcamp

Learnt to write SQL code for database querying, brushed up on my statistics, and completed a group project which involved analyzing the impact of social media on small businesses in Nigeria.

Nov '20

More Projects and Becoming Visible

Worked on my first end-to-end project in Power BI. Made a video presentation of insights gained and shared on LinkedIn. Created first version of my web portfolio and secured two job offers in March ’21.

May '21

Getting Certified

Began training towards getting certified as an Associate Big Data Analyst by Data Science Council of America (DASCA). Presently learning to use Hadoop, Linux OS, R and Tableau for big data analytics tasks.
